About Us

About This Platform

Empowering people with real-time air quality insights and AI-driven health recommendations.

Our Goal

The primary goal of this platform is to raise awareness about air quality and its impact on human health while providing clear, actionable guidance to users. By combining real-time AQI data with AI-powered insights, we aim to help people make better daily decisions—such as when to go outside, take precautions, or limit exposure to polluted air.

This project focuses on education, prevention, and informed decision-making, especially for people living in areas affected by air pollution.

How We Get AQI Data

We fetch air quality and weather-related data using the OpenWeather API. This API provides reliable, real-time information such as:

Air Quality Index

Real-time AQI measurements for your specific location.

Pollutant Levels

PM2.5, PM10, CO, NO₂, SO₂, and O₃ concentrations.

Weather Parameters

Temperature, humidity, and wind speed data.

Location-Based

Data retrieved dynamically based on your coordinates.

Data Collection Methodology

AQI data is collected through official air quality monitoring stations operated by:

Monitoring Organizations

  • Government agencies
  • Environmental organizations
  • Certified weather and pollution monitoring centers

Each monitoring station measures pollutant concentrations using specialized sensors. AQI values are only available for locations where these monitoring stations exist.

Important Limitation

If a city or region does not have a nearby monitoring center, precise AQI data may not be available for that area. This is a known and accepted limitation of air quality monitoring worldwide.

Privacy & Data

We take user privacy seriously.

Our Privacy Commitment

  • Your location data is used only temporarily to fetch AQI information.
  • We do not store, save, or track your location history.
  • All location data exists only during the active session.
  • No personal data is collected or shared with third parties.
