What is AQI?

AQI (Air Quality Index) is a number that shows how clean or polluted the air is around you. It helps you understand whether the air is safe to breathe and how it may affect your health. A lower AQI means cleaner air, while a higher AQI means more pollution and higher health risk‚Äîespecially for children, elderly people, and those with breathing or heart conditions.
